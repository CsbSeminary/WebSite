Exodus

Available for both College or Seminary level

2 credits

Tuition: $640

Audit Fee:  $100

9:00 am – 4:00 pm

Instructor:  Dr. Mark Rooker

Dr. Mark Rooker (PhD, Brandeis Univ.), is professor of Old Testament and Hebrew at Southeastern Baptist Theological Seminary. Dr. Rooker was an editor and translator for the Holman Christian Standard Bible and has published books on the Hebrew language, an Old Testament introduction, the Ten Commandments, and commentaries on the books of Leviticus (NAC) and Ezekiel (HOTC). His second commentary in Ezekiel in the "Evangelical Exegetical Commentary" series (Logos) is forthcoming in 2016.

1T1312 Christian World View

Available for College level only

3 credits

Tuition: $900

Audit Fee:  $150 (need high school degree to audit)

January 19 – May 1, 2015 | Tuesdays | 8am – Noon

 

This course encourages Christians to “think Christianly” by learning what it means to be a Christian disciple with a Christian worldview and learning how to detect and evaluate non-Christian worldviews A central goal is to facilitate students in learning how to think even more than what to think.
