2014 Dr. Garry Owen M.D. Memorial Lectureship

We will be hosting Dr. Donald Sunukjian from January 21-22 for our 2014 Academic Lectureship in memory of Dr. Garry Owen, M.D. Please register online for this upcoming event:

Dr. Sunukjian’s lifelong love has been preaching, both as a pastor and a teacher. His passion is to see God’s Word presented with accuracy, clarity, interest, and relevance.

Lectureship schedule features...

Tuesday, January 21
(10:00am) If You Don't Study for the Test, Can You Still Ask God for Wisdom?
(12:30pm) For Pastors/Students: Moving from Bible Study to Sermon Outline
(2:30pm) How To Make Sure Your Faith Holds Up Through Difficult Times
(7:00pm) Evening Worship and Sermon: "Don't Leave Home Without It"

Wednesday, January 22
(10:00am) Since 'Not Many Should Presume To Be Teachers', Should You Steer Clear of a Preaching Ministry?
(12:00pm) Pastors' Luncheon (FREE for all Pastors)
(2:30pm) Why Doesn't Our 'Prayer Of Faith' Heal the Sick?

The event is FREE to the public, and one credit hour may be earned for registered college and seminary students.
