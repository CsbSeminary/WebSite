CSBS&C Preview Weekend (March 14-15)

What's the weekend all about?


We're so excited that you'll be joining us! If you're thinking about college or seminary, this is a weekend you don't want to miss. Here's our full schedule, which features a Youth Rally on Friday night for Grades 7 and up, and a Preview Day on Saturday for Grades 10 and up:

YOUTH RALLY :: Friday, March 14
(5:00pm) Registration for those staying over Friday night
(7:00pm) Icebreakers
Welcome Message (Dr. Blackaby, CSBS&C President)
(7:30pm) Message and Worship
(8:30pm) Sharing God Stories
(9:00pm) Worship Concert!
(10-11:00pm) Fellowship n' Snacks

(FREE overnight housing is available on campus through registration)

PREVIEW DAY :: Saturday, March 15*
(8:00am) Free Breakfast
(9:00am) Icebreakers
(9:30am) Devotional and Announcements
(10:00am) Worship
(10:30am) Group 1 Campus Tour / Sample Lecture 1**
(11:00am) Group 2 Campus Tour / Sample Lecture 2**
(11:00am) Free Time
(12:00pm) Free Lunch
(1:00pm) Free Time
(3:00pm) Worship
(3:30pm) Message and Worship

*Bring outdoor gear for tobogganing (weather permitting)
**Lecture topics to be determined



The entire weekend is FREE, but you'll need to make sure that you signup through our registration page. If you're coming with your church to just the Youth Rally on Friday night, make sure that your youth group leader knows you've registered!

BONUS :: Still not sure about coming on Saturday? We have an amazing gift package everyone receives, which includes a $100 tuition voucher, school swag, a t-shirt with a missions quote, and more!

Remember to register to make sure we can get you a t-shirt, and feel free to invite your friends!
