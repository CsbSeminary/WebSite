2014 Winter Intensives

A snippet from our upcoming intensive, "Surprised by Jack":

Are you a closet fan of C.S. Lewis? Do you long to know more about the man behind the Shadowlands? Join fellow "Inklings" for a week of learning and participate in a 15-hour January Intensive!

Elaine Philips, adjunct instructor at Canadian Southern Baptist Seminary & College, will focus on the literary and theological contributions of Lewis, highlighting the many genres in which he wrote. Learn about the man behind the name. Sample excerpts from his works. Delight yourself in stories again. (Re)ignite your passion for words. Find the Joy.

There's a whole world waiting beyond the wardrobe...
