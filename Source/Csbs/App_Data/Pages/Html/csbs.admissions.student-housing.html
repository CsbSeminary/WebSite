There are 45 unfurnished housing units available on campus for full-time students only. Most of the units are 3-bedroom apartments with a few 1-bedroom and several 2-bedroom apartments. Information may be obtained through the Housing Director. Students applying for seminary housing will be placed on a waiting list in the order in which their seminary application is approved.

Information and application forms may be obtained through the Housing Office.
