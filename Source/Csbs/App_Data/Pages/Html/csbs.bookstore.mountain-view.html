Welcome to the MountainView Christian Bookstore, located at the Canadian Southern Baptist Seminary & College, 200 Seminary View, in scenic Cochrane, AB.

Our bookstore is open to the public; all are welcome!

Feast your senses on books in a beautiful setting.
We sell an assortment of books, Bibles, gift-ware and cards for the general public, as well as resource books, commentaries and textbooks for students.

We special order Sunday School curriculum, children ministry supplies, small group and Discipleship Member books and church supplies (communion cups, offering envelopes, etc).

Clergy: come on in and receive a 10% discount.
