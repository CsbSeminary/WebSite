The Certificate of Christian Studies is designed for those who want to mature as a follower of Christ, as well as deepen their understanding of the Bible and the teachings of Christ.

Requirements

  1. The prerequisite for this certificate is a high school diploma or GED equivalent
  2. A total of 24 credit hours is required for the completion of the certificate.

Objectives

Students who complete the Certificate of Christian Studies will be better equipped to:

  1. Serve the Lord from a solid foundation of Bible knowledge
  2. Discern and follow God’s direction for their lives
  3. Put into practice the principles of spiritual growth.
