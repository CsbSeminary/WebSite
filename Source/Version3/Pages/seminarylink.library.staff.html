Kathy Seidler

Library Director

Kathy Seidler was born and raised in Edmonton, AB, and earned her B.A. from East Texas Baptist University; MLIS from the University of Alberta; and her Master of Divinity from CSBS. She began as Library Director at CSBS in August 1990, and has served in that capacity since that time, excluding a three-year year hiatus from 1995-1998. Kathy is married to Herb Seidler and is the mother of three children: Mac, Jack, and Kennnedy. She is a member of Bow Valley Baptist Church, where she serves in the babies class, PowerPoint Ministry, sound operator, and sings special music.
