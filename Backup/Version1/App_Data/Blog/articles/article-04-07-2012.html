CSBS&C's 25th Anniversary Celebration!

Thank you for joining us on August 24-25 for our 25th Anniversary Celebration! Hundreds connected to CSBS&C took part in the weekend festivities, which featured live bands, carnival games, food and fellowship.

Look forward to the upcoming edition of SeminaryNews (Fall 2012) and a CSBS&C 25th Anniversary Booklet for a history of our school's past twenty-five years, and a recap of the celebration weekend.
